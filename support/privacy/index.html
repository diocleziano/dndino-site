    Privacy¶

    Last updated: April 9, 2026

    DnDino is a macOS app designed to help Dungeon Masters manage adventures, characters, places, combats, maps, and session notes.

    Data collection¶

    DnDino does not collect personal data for advertising, profiling, or tracking purposes.

    Data entered into the app, such as adventures, characters, notes, images, and related content, is stored locally on the user's device.

    Local storage¶

    Most data handled by DnDino is stored locally on the user's Mac within the app's data container.

    iCloud¶

    If the user chooses to use iCloud-based sync or snapshot features, some app data may be stored in the user's iCloud account solely to enable backup, synchronization, or restoration of content.

    DnDino does not access the user's iCloud data for any purpose other than providing the requested app functionality.

    Images and user-selected files¶

    DnDino may allow users to select images or files from their device. These files are used only within the app for the features requested by the user.

    Tracking and third parties¶

    DnDino does not use advertising tracking systems and does not sell personal data to third parties.

    Security¶

    Data is handled using the standard security mechanisms provided by macOS and Apple technologies used by the app.

    Contact¶

    If you have questions about privacy or data handling related to DnDino, you can contact:

    dndino.app@gmail.com
