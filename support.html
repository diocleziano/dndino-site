DnDino Support

DnDino is a macOS app designed for Dungeon Masters who want to organize everything they need for a campaign in one place.

What DnDino can do

DnDino helps you prepare and run tabletop RPG adventures by giving you a single workspace for campaign material, session notes, places, characters, monsters, combats, and maps.

Main features include:

  • Adventure management with descriptions, notes, and campaign structure
  • Places and quests with separate information for Dungeon Master and players
  • Characters, NPCs, and monsters with detailed records
  • Combat management with initiative, hit points, conditions, and quick actions
  • Interactive maps with linked markers for places
  • Concept maps for connecting ideas, locations, and story elements
  • Rich text notes with internal links between characters, places, and spells
  • Session tools for live play, quick notes, and campaign continuity
  • Search tools for quickly finding important content during play
  • Local backup and optional iCloud snapshot support
